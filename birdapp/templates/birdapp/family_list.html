About Bird Families

What are Bird Families?

Bird families are taxonomic groups that organize related species. For example, all ducks, geese, and swans belong to the family Anatidae, while all hawks, eagles, and kites belong to Accipitridae.

Why Use Families?

Organizing birds by family helps you:
• Better understand bird relationships
• Filter and search your bird database
• Learn taxonomic classification
• Organize your sightings scientifically
